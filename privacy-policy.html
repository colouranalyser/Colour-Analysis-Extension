Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Colour Analyser does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, browsing history, or the colours you analyse. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

Colour Analyser is a browser extension that helps you identify whether clothing and product colours suit your seasonal colour palette. This policy explains what data the extension uses, how it is stored, and your rights.

Data stored on your device

The following data is stored in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party:

  • Your selected colour season (e.g. Soft Autumn, True Winter)
  • Your daily colour pick count and the date it was last reset
  • Your paid or free status, cached locally after verification
  • The position of the extension panel on your screen

This data exists solely to preserve your preferences between browsing sessions. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or uninstalling the extension.

Data collected when you purchase

If you choose to upgrade to a paid plan, your payment is processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. At that point, the following data is collected by those services:

  • Your email address
  • Your payment information, stored securely by Stripe

We do not store or have access to your payment card details. ExtensionPay and Stripe have their own privacy policies governing this data.

What we do not collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to:

  • The colours you pick or analyse
  • The websites you visit
  • Your browsing history
  • Product pages or shopping behaviour
  • Any personal information beyond what is listed above

Third-party services

ExtensionPay — used to manage paid subscriptions and one-time purchases. Their privacy policy is available at extensionpay.com.

Stripe — used to process payments. Their privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

We use no advertising networks, analytics platforms, tracking pixels, or any other third-party data collection services.

Data retention

Locally stored preferences persist until you clear your browser data or uninstall the extension. Payment records are retained by ExtensionPay and Stripe in accordance with their policies and applicable financial regulations.

Your rights

You may request deletion of any account data associated with your email address by contacting us at the address below. You may clear locally stored preferences at any time through your browser settings.

Changes to this policy

We will update this policy if our data practices change. The updated date at the top of this document reflects the most recent revision.
